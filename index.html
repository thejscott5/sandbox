HOW DOES FIRST HYPERION INTEGRATE?

Through the acquisition, the FIRST HYPERION structure would provide a management framework that begins with a centralized SUPPORT cell. This approach would grant a sustainable model focused on data, generated by PEOPLE, PROCESS, and SYSTEM.

  • SOURCING

    Leads relationships with the suppliers
    Choose suppliers with the best selection and performance rates, matching the best DPOs to reliable partnerships, then concentrating to few strategic companies.

  • PRODUCTS

    Designs inventory carried and which products are bought/sold
    Within customer base niches, finding the basket of products that showed the highest similarities, and thus most likely higher probability to be bought within niche (NAICS).

  • OPERATIONS

    Designs the operation of stock, blends and preparations
    Products analyzed to an accurate stratification, having their GMROII as the most relevant factor. Inventory reflects the new commercial strategy. Process items in the leanest way possible.

  • SALES

    Leads existing and new customer partnerships
    Looking for the most profitable niche (NAICS) and gaining an industry-focused approach. The goal is to increase share of wallet, with organic growth. Those customers that also provide best DSOs.

  • LOGISTICS

    Designs the fleet to move products in and out.
    Define a radius of Service, which would be ideally within a 90-mile distance, affording at most 150 miles. Focusing on the customer base in that range.

  • SUPPORT

    Designs the necessary structure for all other to operate.
    We have a successfully demonstrated distribution model which is repeatable and scalable. The model can be applied to an opportunity in a currently fragmented market.
