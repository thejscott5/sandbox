First Hyperion is committed to building lasting partnerships in distribution by remaining true to who we are and what we believe.

Through a decentralized leadership approach, we unleash the entrepreneurial spirit of people pursuing their own wealth creation while preserving the invaluable legacy of family-owned businesses. At the core of First Hyperion’s business philosophy, we believe that shared success resides at the intersection of values and process.

  • Own Safety

    Going above and beyond to keep everyone safe
    See something, do something. Never put growth or profit ahead of safety. Invest in best-in-class training, processes, and equipment. Continually reduce and eliminate risks

    Always act with the best of intentions. Own your actions: Admit your mistakes, fix it and learn from it. Tell the truth at all times, no matter the consequences. Admit your mistakes, fix it and learn from it.

  • Win Together

    Victory through partnership
    Listen to understand. Recognize and celebrate success. Collaborate to overcome challenges and win. Create opportunities for mutual gain.

  • Execute Exceptionally

    Create value through a shared commitment to excellence
    Know your role, goals, and responsibilities. Continuously measure the metrics that matter. Relentless pursuit of self-improvement. Take initiative to exceed customer expectations. Bring Enthusiasm.
